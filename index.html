The Warhammer 40K Universe

Warhammer 40,000 is a dystopian science fantasy universe set in the far future. Humanity is ruled by the Imperium of Man under the immortal Emperor. Endless wars rage across millions of worlds against xenos races, traitors, and daemonic horrors.

Massive armies, gothic starships, ancient relics, and cosmic horrors define the setting. Every battle shapes the fate of the galaxy.
